# Cowork Task: Pre-Call Brief
# Usage: On-demand, 30 minutes before a call
# Fill in [Name] and [Company] before running

I have a call with [Name] at [Company] in 30 minutes.

1. Read their row in my Notion Contacts DB — pull their Role, Status, What They've Done For Me, Notes, Follow-Up Date, Last Interaction.
2. Read any linked Calls DB entries for this contact — what did we talk about before?
3. Search Exa for recent news about [Company] from the last 3 months.
4. Search Exa for [Name]'s LinkedIn profile or any public professional info.

Write a 5-bullet brief:
- Who they are and what they do (role, company, how we connected)
- What we've discussed before (from Calls DB) — or "First conversation" if none
- Recent company context worth knowing (from Exa search)
- 3 specific questions I should ask in this call
- My goal for this call based on their status in my system (what outcome would make this call a win)

Keep it under 200 words. Don't pad. If Exa finds nothing, say so and skip that bullet.

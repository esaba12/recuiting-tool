Read my Notion Applications DB — all rows with stage changes this week or Last Activity in the past 7 days.
Read my Notion LC Problems DB — all rows where Solved Date is in the past 7 days.
Check Google Calendar for any recruiting events or interviews that occurred this week.

Write a recruiting memo with exactly 5 sections:

**Section 1: Week in Numbers**
- Calls made (Calls DB rows created this week)
- New contacts added (Contacts DB rows created this week)
- Emails processed (estimate from Applications DB activity)
- LC problems solved (count + breakdown by difficulty)

**Section 2: Hot Right Now**
- Contacts with Urgency = HIGH
- Pending offers or interview decisions
- Upcoming interviews (from calendar) in the next 7 days
- Active conversations where a reply is overdue

**Section 3: Going Cold**
- Contacts where Last Interaction > 14 days ago and Status is not Closed
- Applications where Days in Stage > 14 and Stage is Applied or Phone Screen
- Flag anyone who was "Warm" 2 weeks ago and is now cooling

**Section 4: LeetCode Progress**
- Topics solved this week (list the patterns)
- Total problems in "Due for Review" queue right now
- Weakest area based on recent activity (fewest problems, lowest success rate if visible)
- Recommendation: what to focus on next week

**Section 5: Next Week's Priorities**
List maximum 5 actions, ranked by importance. Be specific — include names, companies, and what to do. 
No vague "follow up with contacts" — say "Email Sarah at Stripe re: phone screen confirmation."

Save as 'Recruiting Memo — Week of [Monday date in format "June 30, 2026"].md' in my Google Drive Recruiting/Memos folder (create it if it doesn't exist).
